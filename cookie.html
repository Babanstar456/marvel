01 — Introduction

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

When you visit marvex.in (our "Website"), we may place cookies or access cookies already stored on your device. We also use similar technologies such as pixel tags, web beacons, and local storage, which function in a comparable way to cookies.

ℹ

Your control matters. You can choose to accept, decline, or manage specific types of cookies at any time. Declining certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

02 — Categories

Types of cookies we use

We categorise the cookies on our website into four types based on their purpose. Essential cookies are always active; all others can be controlled through your browser settings or our cookie preferences.

Essential Cookies
Required
These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They enable core features like page navigation, secure areas, and form submissions. The website cannot operate properly without them.
Session management Security Form state
Performance Cookies
Optional
These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website — pages visited, time spent, errors encountered. All data is aggregated and anonymised. They help us improve how our website works.
Page analytics Error tracking Load times
Functional Cookies
Optional
These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your language or region) and provide enhanced, personalised features. They may be set by us or by third-party providers.
Preferences Language Region
Marketing Cookies
Optional
These cookies track your browsing habits to deliver advertising more relevant to you and your interests. They are usually placed by advertising networks with our permission and may share data with other organisations.
Retargeting Ad tracking Conversion

Third-party cookies

Some cookies on our website are placed by third-party services we use for analytics, advertising, and embedded content. We do not have direct control over third-party cookies — they are governed by the respective third party's privacy and cookie policies.

Third-party services currently used on our website that may place cookies:

  • Google Analytics — Website usage analytics to understand visitor behaviour and improve our content. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Google Ads (Tag Manager) — Conversion tracking and remarketing for our paid advertising campaigns. Google Ads Policy →

We periodically review the third-party services we use and will update this policy accordingly. If you have concerns about a specific third-party, we recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies directly.

06 — Consent

Your consent

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie notice that allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. By clicking "Accept All", you consent to the placement of all cookies described in this policy.

Your consent preferences are stored in a functional cookie (cookie_consent) for up to 12 months so that we do not need to ask you again on subsequent visits.

You may withdraw or change your consent at any time by:

  • Clearing your browser cookies and refreshing our website to reset your preferences
  • Adjusting your browser settings to block specific categories of cookies
  • Contacting us directly at office.marvex@gmail.com to request preference changes

Our legal basis for processing data collected via cookies is your consent (where obtained) and our legitimate interests in understanding how our website is used and delivering relevant advertising to potential clients.
